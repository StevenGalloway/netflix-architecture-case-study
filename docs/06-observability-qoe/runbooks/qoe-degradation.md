# Runbook: QoE Degradation

## Detection
- Elevated TTFF
- Rebuffer anomalies
- Customer complaints

## Immediate Mitigation
1. Shift traffic from impacted regions/CDNs
2. Reduce bitrate ladder
3. Scale critical services

## Investigation
- Inspect QoE dashboards
- Correlate traces & logs
- Identify failing dependencies

## Recovery
- Confirm SLO restoration
- Monitor for 30 minutes
- Document findings
