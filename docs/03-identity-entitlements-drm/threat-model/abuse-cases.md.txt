# Abuse Cases: Identity & DRM

## Token Replay Attack
Stolen playback token reused on unauthorized devices.

**Mitigation:** Short-lived, device-bound tokens.

## Credential Stuffing
Automated login attempts.

**Mitigation:** Rate limiting, bot detection, MFA.

## License Scraping
Harvesting DRM licenses for offline cracking.

**Mitigation:** Device fingerprinting, behavioral detection, DRM policy enforcement.
